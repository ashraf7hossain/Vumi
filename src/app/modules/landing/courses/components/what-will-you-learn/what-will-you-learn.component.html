WHAT YOU WILL LEARN

  • Recognize different styles of communication and how to improve understanding and build rapport with others,
  • Analyze situations using a variety of communication models to determine how to manage the situations,
  • Understand the insights of the counterparty and enrich communication skills they need to become valued and successful network participants
  • Determine the most effective match of messages to the unique circumstances in particular situations
  • Learn how to persuade counterparties effectively
  • Learn interpersonal communication, group dynamics, conflict resolution and negotiation to go a long way toward making meetings more effective
  • Evaluate personal communication, verbal and non-verbal, formal and informal, to identify specific areas for improvement
  • Design organizational communication strategy that effectively uses presentations, reports, and mass communication
  • Evaluate ethical issues in all forms of communication within organizations
