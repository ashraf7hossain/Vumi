OVERVIEW

OF THE COURSE

Communication is a critical skill for every managers to be successful in their professional career. This course Effective Managerial Communication is designed to help managers/leaders communicate effectively in their everyday tasks and intended to help them think strategically about com- munication and aid them in improving their writing, presentation, and interpersonal communication skills as a future business leader. This course will also cover emotional intelligence (EQ), its importance and strategies to increase EQ awareness and management. This course also covers vari- ous negotiation styles and tactics. This course will enable a manager by teaching him/her the art of persuasion. This course is very effective for learning the key strategies and tactics needed for effective written & oral communications and winning negotiations with employees, supervisors, customers & stakeholders.
